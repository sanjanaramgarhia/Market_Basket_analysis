What is Apriori Algorithm?

Apriori algorithm finds sets of items that appear together frequently, and then builds rules like If a customer buys X, they also buy Y.

It helps you find patterns, co-occurring items, and hidden relations in large datasets â€” mainly used in market basket analysis.

Why it is called Apriori?
Because it uses a-priori knowledge:
  If an itemset is frequent, then all its subsets must also be frequent.
  If a subset is not frequent, the larger set containing it cannot be frequent.
